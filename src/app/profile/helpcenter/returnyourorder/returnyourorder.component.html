Can we return the order ?

You may initiate a return for your order; however, specific conditions apply. Please ensure that the products you wish to return are eligible based on the product description or your "My Order" section, as they must be listed there for a return to be processed.

What are terms and conditions for returning the order ?

Our terms and conditions assure customers that they will receive a full refund in case of product damage or defects. Furthermore, if any damage occurs during the logistics process, a full refund will still be provided. This guarantees a secure shopping experience for our valued customers.

What is the process to return an order ?

To initiate a product return, please navigate to the "My Orders" section, select the relevant product, and click on the "Return Product" option. Subsequently, provide the reason for the return. Our dedicated team will promptly contact you to gather comprehensive details, assess the situation, and initiate the return process accordingly.

How much time does it takes to initiate the refund ?

The refund process typically takes 7 days for domestic transactions and 7-11 days for international transactions. Please note that the exact time frame may vary slightly depending on your payment method and banking institution. Rest assured, we strive to expedite the process to ensure you receive your refund as swiftly as possible.
