My Orders

Upon placing your order, it will promptly appear in the "My Orders" section, where you will find a comprehensive overview of essential details pertaining to your invoice and bill. Additionally, for your convenience, real-time shipment tracking can be accessed through the dedicated "Live Tracking" section within the order details.

What to do if order is placed for ex-work delivery ?

After placing your ex-work order, submit the required docket documents and numbers. After verification, pay any outstanding amount, if applicable. Then, view images of your packed order. Finally, your authorized partner collects the shipment from our specified warehouse. This ensures a seamless ex-work delivery process.

How much time it will take to deliver the order ?

Delivery times vary depending on the destination, domestic or international. For domestic orders, the standard delivery period is around one week. If you need expedited delivery, please (contact) our team. For international shipments, delivery times are based on the chosen shipping method and partner. You'll receive estimated delivery times accordingly. Order tracking is available for your convenience.

What is order Delivered and On the way

An order has been dispatched to your designated delivery location, and you have confirmed the receipt of the shipment. The current status indicates that the order has been dispatched from the warehouse and is en route to your specified destination. It is expected to arrive at your doorstep shortly.

Where can you find the Docket document and Docket number ?

For ex-work shipments, access your Docket document and Docket number in your order confirmation email or online account. This information is essential for tracking your delivery.

    • "Ex Works" means the seller's responsibility ends once the product is made available at their premises. You'll need to arrange and pay for all shipping and customs-related expenses.
    • The estimated delivery time is usually mentioned in the order confirmation email. It depends on the shipping method and your location.
    • Depending on the order status, you may or may not be able to change the shipping address. Contact our customer support for assistance.
    • Customs duties may apply depending on your country's regulations. For DDP orders, duties are included. For EXW orders, you'll be responsible for customs clearance.
    • Shipping method changes might be possible if your order hasn't shipped yet. Contact us to inquire about changing the shipping method.
    • Unfortunately, once an order is placed, we can't modify the items. You may need to cancel and place a new order.
