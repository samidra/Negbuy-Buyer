What is docket number and where to find docket number?

  • A docket number is a unique identifier crucial for shipments in ecommerce import-export.
  • It streamlines customs procedures and optimizes logistics operations.
  • This number is included in shipping documentation for easy tracking of goods.
  • It ensures compliance with regulatory requirements by linking documentation to specific shipments.
  • Docket numbers aid in meticulous record-keeping and provide transparency to customers about order status.
